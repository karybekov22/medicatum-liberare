1. Users can log in to their accounts using their email and password or sign up if they don't have an account.
2. Registering users mobile app collects their name, births of date, mobile phone number and payment credentials.
3. Users can select a good from a catalog of items and place an order, also they should mention an adress,
  if they have enough funds on their accounts and if a good is available in stock.
4. If a good is not available in stock users promted editing an order or canceling it.
5. If there is not enough funds available in user's accounts than user is informed.
6. Then, funds are withdrawn and placed to packing and delivering.
7. Receiving the delivery users can evaluate the service and leave a comment.
8. Also, an invoice is send to user's email.
